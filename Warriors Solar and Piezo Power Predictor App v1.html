Warriors Solar and Piezo Power Predictor App

Created by warriors group which are CpE students of Jose Rizal University.This app predicts the solar and piezo power output based on the input temperature and humidity. Dependent and applicable for the prototype’s specifications: Solar Panel (polycrystalline, 88*142mm, 5V, 2W) and Piezo Electric transducer (35mm, 12 pcs)
