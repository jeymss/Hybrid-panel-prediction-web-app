Warriors Solar and Piezo Power Predictor App

Created by warriors group which are CpE students of Jose Rizal University. This app predicts the solar and piezo power output, as well as the solar panel dimension and the number of piezoelectric transducers needed to generate a specific power output based on the input temperature, humidity and power output needed. Dependent and applicable for the prototype’s specifications: Solar Panel (polycrystalline, 88*142mm, 5V, 2W) and Piezo Electric transducer (35mm, 12 pcs).
